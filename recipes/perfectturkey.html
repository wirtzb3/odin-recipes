Perfect Turkey

Description

An easy, tasty, succulent, moist turkey that will be the highlight of the table!

Ingredients

  • 1 18lb turkey
  • 2 cups kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 4 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 4 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup white wine

Steps

  1. Rub the turkey with koshetr salt and place in large pot. Cover with cold water and brine for 12 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 350, discard the brine and wash the turkey.
  3. Brush the turkey with half the melted butter. Stuff the turkey with vegetables. Cover with white wine and put any extra vegetables in the bottom of a roasting pan.
  4. Roast uncovered 3.5 to 4 hours. Turn the bird 2/3 of the way through. Thigh needs to reach 180 degrees.
