Grandma's Green Bean Casserole

Description

A crowd favorite, much better than the traditional onion topped one. It's melty and gooey and has probably 1000 calories per square!

Ingredients

  • 2 T butter
  • 2 T flour
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 t white sugar
  • 1/4 cup onion
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3 14.5 oz cans French style green beans, drained
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup Ritz crackers
  • 1 T butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Melt 2 T butter in skillet, stir in flour and stir until smooth, cook for one minute. Stir in salt, sugar, sour cream, onions, and green beans. Stir to coat.
  3. Transfer to a 8x8 pan. Spread cheese over. Mix melted butter with crushed Ritz, sprinkle over the top.
  4. Bake for 30 minurtes or until Ritz are browned and cheese is bubbly.
