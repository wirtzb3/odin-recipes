Celery Stuffing

Description

This stuffing is celery and onion filled! You won't get enough of all those yummy nutrients, along with a ton of carbs!

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf sliced white bread
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 t poultry seasoning
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Steps

  1. Let bread air dry, then cut into cubes.
  2. Melt butter, and cook onion and celery until soft. Season with salt, pepper, and poultry seasoning. Stir in bread cubes until evenly coated. Moisten with chicken broth and mix well.
  3. Bake in a casserole dish at 350 for 30 to 40 minutes.
